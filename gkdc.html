Go Kart Design and Fabrication

Design of a vehicle involves optimization to ensure that a feature that enhances one objective does not weaken another. After a thorough study of go-karts, their functioning and key features, the design process commenced and concluded with a model that met the requirements. The frame is the basic skeleton of the kart. It is designed to withstand the stresses of the kart under static and dynamic conditions. The chassis is to accommodate all components of the cart including the driver, efficiently and safely.

As a memeber of the design team, we had to consider the following factors. The chassis has to be light as well as rigid enough to support the loads acting on it. Lighter karts result in better power-to-weight ratios. Mere design and analysis is insufficient to create a successful product. The ease with which it can be manufactured is crucial. The kart has been designed keeping this in mind. It has been done by modelling the kart using SOLIDWORKS and analyzing it using ANSYS. The pipes are designed as per the available standard dimension. The weldments have been reduced and replaced by bending to connect two pipes. This reduces the number of weak components and strengthens the frame.





The technical requirements of an IC Engine powered go-kart were identified and incorporated into our vehicle. Design software and analysis tools were utilized to optimize the model for better performance, safety, comfort and aesthetics.
